Wisp HTTP Client with TLS - Browser Example

🔒 Security Notice

Genuine End-to-End Encryption: This client implements TLS 1.3 encryption client-side. HTTPS requests are encrypted by your browser before being sent through the Wisp server, ensuring the Wisp server cannot see your data - just like native browser HTTPS.
